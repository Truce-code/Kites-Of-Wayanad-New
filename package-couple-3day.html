Choose Your Adventure for Day 2 & 3

Select one of the following plans for the rest of your trip.

Plan A: Heritage Plan B: Lakes & Dams Plan C: Adventure

Plan A: Nature & Heritage Escape

  • Morning: Private Waterfall Hike (Budget Category only) & Breakfast.
  • Explore Edakkal Caves (Closed Mondays) & Phantom Rock Trek.
  • Visit Karapuzha Dam or Nellarachal View Point.
  • Drive to Kozhikode Beach & Sunset (Time permitting).
  • Shopping at Commercial Street & Drop-off at Railway Station.

Plan B: Lakes, Dams & Waterfalls

  • Morning: Private Waterfall Hike & Breakfast.
  • Visit massive Banasura Sagar Dam (Speed boating available).
  • Enjoy Karlad Lake or view Meenmutty Waterfalls.
  • Drive to Kozhikode Beach & Sunset (Time permitting).
  • Shopping at Commercial Street & Drop-off at Railway Station.

Plan C: Adventure & Thrill Seekers

  • Morning: Private Waterfall Hike & Breakfast.
  • Experience Longest Zipline & Zip Cycling.
  • 900 Kandi Off-Road Jeep Safari & Glass Bridge Walk.
  • Drive to Kozhikode Beach & Sunset (Time permitting).
  • Shopping at Commercial Street & Drop-off at Railway Station.
