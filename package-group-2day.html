Day 1

Arrival & Exploration

Lakkidi & Pookode
  • Pickup from Kalpetta/Kozhikode & Travel to Wayanad.
  • Fresh up & Breakfast (Self-Sponsored). (Optional: Tusharagiri Falls for Kozhikode pickup only).
  • Visit Lakkidi View Point & Pookode Lake / Vythiri Ultra Park.
  • Enroute En Ooru Tribal Village (Advance Booking Mandatory).
  • Visit Tea Museum / Honey Museum & Shopping.
  • Check-in to Homestay/Camping. Dinner & Campfire.

Choose Your Plan For Day 2

Select one option for your group.

Plan A: Heritage Plan B: Dams & Lakes Plan C: Adventure

Plan A: Caves & Falls

  • Wake-up call & Breakfast.
  • Edakkal Caves (Easy to Moderate Trek).
  • Lunch (Self-sponsored).
  • Visit Kanthanpara Falls or Karapuzha Dam.
  • Enroute Kozhikode Beach/Sunset (Tentative) & Commercial Street.
  • Drop at Kozhikode Railway Station.

Plan B: Water Wonders

  • Wake-up call & Breakfast.
  • Enroute Banasura Sagar Dam (Largest earth dam in India).
  • Lunch (Self-sponsored).
  • Visit Karlad Lake or Meenmutty Falls.
  • Enroute Kozhikode Beach/Sunset (Tentative) & Commercial Street.
  • Drop at Kozhikode Railway Station.

Plan C: Pure Adventure

  • Wake-up call & Breakfast.
  • 900 Kandi Off-road Jeep Safari & Glass Bridge.
  • Longest Zipline, Zip Cycling & Digital Glass Bridge.
  • Lunch (Self-sponsored).
  • Enroute Kozhikode Beach/Sunset (Tentative) & Commercial Street.
  • Drop at Kozhikode Railway Station.
