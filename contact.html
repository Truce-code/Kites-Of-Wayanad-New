Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide pick-up from the railway station?

Yes! We can arrange pick-up from nearby railway stations like Kozhikode (Calicut) or Mysore upon request.

Is food included in the package?

Usually, food is not included to give you the flexibility to try various local cuisines, but we can include it in a custom package.

Can we customize the itinerary?

Absolutely! The 4-day plan is our best-seller, but we are happy to tailor the trip to your specific needs.
